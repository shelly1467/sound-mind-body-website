About Sound Mind & Body

I’m Shelly, an affirmation and motivational music creator with a heart for helping people reconnect with their inner strength, calm their minds, and create more peace in their everyday lives.

Sound Mind & Body began as a love for positive encouragement, soulful music, and the healing power of words. Over time, it grew into a creative space designed to support emotional wellness through affirmations, peaceful visuals, soothing sound, and gentle reminders that you are not alone.

Every track, message, and creative piece is thoughtfully made to help you slow down, breathe deeply, and reconnect with yourself. By blending motivational affirmations with calming melodies and uplifting energy, Sound Mind & Body was created to be more than music — it is a supportive experience for the mind, body, and spirit.

Whether you are starting your morning with intention, journaling, meditating, practicing self-care, relaxing after a long day, working out, or simply searching for encouragement during difficult moments, this space was created to meet you where you are.

The Heart Behind Sound Mind & Body

This space wasn’t built out of a wellness trend. It was built out of necessity.

Living with Multiple Sclerosis, I quickly learned how important it is to protect my mental peace and find moments of calm in the middle of the unpredictable. I know what it feels like to search for a quiet anchor when life feels overwhelming.

That is why I created Sound Mind & Body — as a gentle place for anyone who needs a moment to step away from the noise, choose an intention, and return to themselves.

My hope is that when you arrive here, you feel supported, encouraged, and reminded that even in a hard moment, peace is still possible.

More than anything, I want this to be a space where you can finally say:

“I can breathe again.”

Our Goal

At Sound Mind & Body, the goal is simple: to create content that helps people feel emotionally supported, mentally refreshed, spiritually grounded, and inspired to keep moving forward.

Through affirmation music, wellness inspiration, mindset support, and peaceful creative experiences, my mission is to remind people that healing, growth, confidence, and inner peace are possible — one moment, one breath, and one affirmation at a time.

Sound Mind & Body is a place to return to peace.
