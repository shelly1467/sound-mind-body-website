Simple practice

How to Use This Music or Affirmation

  1. Place a hand on your heart or breathe with both feet on the floor.
  2. Listen without judging your feelings or forcing positivity.
  3. Repeat the affirmations that feel comforting, even if quietly.
  4. After listening, do one small act of care for yourself.

Begin gently. You do not need to do anything perfectly for affirmation music to become part of a supportive self-care routine.

Questions

FAQ

What are self-love affirmations?

They are positive, compassionate statements that help you practice worthiness, acceptance, and kindness toward yourself.

Can I listen when I feel emotional?

Yes. Keep the volume gentle, breathe slowly, and pause anytime you need more space.

How often should I use them?

Use them as often as they feel supportive, especially during morning routines, bedtime, or moments of self-doubt.
