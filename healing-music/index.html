Simple practice

How to Use This Music or Affirmation

  1. Find a quiet space where you can sit, lie down, or move gently.
  2. Set an intention such as rest, release, forgiveness, or peace.
  3. Let the music play while you breathe naturally and relax your shoulders.
  4. Use healing music before sleep or after a long day to transition into calm.

Begin gently. You do not need to do anything perfectly for affirmation music to become part of a supportive self-care routine.

Questions

FAQ

Can healing music cure illness?

Healing music is not a medical treatment, but it can support relaxation, comfort, and emotional self-care.

Is this good for sleep?

Yes, many people enjoy gentle healing music as part of a calming evening or bedtime routine.

How long should I listen?

Even five to ten minutes can help, but you can listen longer if it feels supportive.
